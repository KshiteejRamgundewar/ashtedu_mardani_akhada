About Us

Heritage of Warriors

Ashtedu Mardani Akhada is dedicated to preserving and promoting the legendary martial traditions of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. For generations, we have cultivated the warrior spirit through authentic training in traditional Maharashtrian combat arts.

Our academy stands as a beacon of cultural pride, blending ancient warrior wisdom with modern training methodologies. We honor the legacy of the Marathi warriors while empowering students with skills applicable to contemporary life.

Our Vision

To create a globally recognized center of excellence in traditional martial arts, fostering discipline, courage, and cultural pride in every student who walks through our doors.
